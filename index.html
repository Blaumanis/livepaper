About Us

Quality

We source our process componenets from high quality materials. Our paper straws are made with FSC & PEFC certified papers that adhere to standards of sustainable forestry. Sustainably Sourced and Food Safe.

Professionalism

From raw material sourcing to manufacturing, quality testing, and packaging, each production stage is handled with the highest care and professionalism to ensure that the best and most hygienic paper straws are delivered to you.

Personalization

We offer our customers great choices. We can customize straws to suit your branding needs and in different diameters. Putting your company logo and colours on drinking straws or wrappers is a great way to get in touch.

Our Mission

THE PAPER STRAWS YOU & OUR ENVIRONMENT CAN DEPEND ON:

LivePaper produces 100% eco-friendly and fully biodegradable paper straws that are safe for enviroment and oceans. Our goal is to replace plastic straws with eco-friendly paper alternatives that do not compromise durability nor enjoyment. We believe that this small change in our drinking habits can lead towards a cleaner environment and future.
